Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS) — License Evidence
====================================================

Source: https://web.stanford.edu/~yesavage/GDS.html
Accessed: 2026-03-21

Public domain statement from the official GDS page maintained by Dr. Jerome
Yesavage at Stanford University / VA Palo Alto Health Care System:

    "The original scale is in the public domain due to it being partly the
    result of Federal support."

The page also notes: "Neither I nor Dr. Brink keep up on every nuance of the
scale but we try to answer questions as work permits."

Regarding translations hosted on the page: "Neither the ACRC or Dr. Yesavage
can vouch for the accuracy of any of the following translations."

The page hosts translations in approximately 40+ languages, many contributed
by researchers at major institutions worldwide.

Basic references:

  Brink TL, Yesavage JA, Lum O, Heersema P, Adey MB, Rose TL: Screening
  tests for geriatric depression. Clinical Gerontologist 1: 37-44, 1982.

  Yesavage JA, Brink TL, Rose TL, Lum O, Huang V, Adey MB, Leirer VO:
  Development and validation of a geriatric depression screening scale: A
  preliminary report. Journal of Psychiatric Research 17: 37-49, 1983.

  Sheikh JI, Yesavage JA: Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS): Recent evidence
  and development of a shorter version. Clinical Gerontology: A Guide to
  Assessment and Intervention 165-173, NY: The Haworth Press, 1986.
