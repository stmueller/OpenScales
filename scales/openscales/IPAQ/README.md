# International Physical Activity Questionnaire Short Form (IPAQ-SF)

7-item self-report measure of physical activity over the past 7 days. Yields raw day and minute counts for vigorous, moderate, and walking activity, plus sitting time. Official MET-minute scoring: Vigorous = 8.0 × days × min/day; Moderate = 4.0 × days × min/day; Walking = 3.3 × days × min/day. Categories: Inactive (<600 MET-min/week), Minimally Active (600–2999), HEPA Active (≥3000 and meets additional criteria). The framework collects raw item responses; apply scoring formula in R/Python.
