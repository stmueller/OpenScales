# Mental Health Inventory (MHI-38) (MHI-38)

38-item measure of psychological distress and well-being developed for general populations. Items use a 6-point frequency scale (All of the time to None of the time) referring to the past month. Yields 5 subscales: Anxiety, Depression, Loss of Behavioral/Emotional Control, General Positive Affect, and Emotional Ties. Two composite scores can be derived: Psychological Distress (Anxiety + Depression + Loss of Control) and Psychological Well-Being (Positive Affect + Emotional Ties). Subscale scores are means (range 1–6); higher scores indicate better mental health. To convert to the standard 0–100 metric, apply: (mean − 1) / 5 × 100.
